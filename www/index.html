The University of Technology, Mauritius (UTM) is a public research university in Mauritius. The main campus lies in La Tour Koenig, Pointe-aux-Sables, within the district of Moka. It was founded following the government of Mauritius approval of the setting up of the University of Technology, Mauritius in January 2000 and the proclamation of The University of Technology, Mauritius Act on 21 June 2000. UTM is a member of the Association of Commonwealth Universities and is listed in the Commonwealth Universities Handbook and in the International Handbook of Universities. UTM is a member of the Southern African Regional Universities Association (SARUA) - a network of public universities in the SADC region.

Toptal STEM Scholarships for Women

Toptal has recently started a scholarship program to award 12 scholarships over the next 12 months to help women pursue their technical career aspirations.

It is open to more than just aspiring developers; the award applies to anyone with a passion for tech and we welcome applicants from all majors and any academic standing.

Each scholarship winner will receive a stipend, and in addition, a year of mentorship and guidance from the senior developers within our network.
